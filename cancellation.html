Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines the cancellation terms for bookings made through PRATA Rentals. Please note that Hosts may select from different cancellation policies, and the specific policy applicable to your booking will be clearly stated on the listing page and during the booking process.

Guest Cancellation

Cancellation deadlines are calculated based on the property's local check-in time (typically 3:00 PM unless specified otherwise) on the day of check-in.

Flexible Policy (Example)

  • Full refund if canceled at least 24 hours before check-in.
  • If canceled less than 24 hours before check-in, the first night is non-refundable.
  • No refund for the remaining nights if the guest decides to leave early after check-in.

Moderate Policy (Example)

  • Full refund if canceled at least 5 days before check-in.
  • If canceled between 5 days and 24 hours before check-in, a 50% refund for all nights.
  • If canceled less than 24 hours before check-in, the first night is non-refundable, and 50% refund for remaining nights.

Strict Policy (Example)

  • Full refund if canceled within 48 hours of booking, provided the cancellation occurs at least 14 days before check-in.
  • 50% refund if canceled at least 7 days before check-in.
  • No refunds for cancellations made within 7 days of check-in.

Host Cancellation

If a Host cancels a confirmed booking, the Guest will receive a full refund. PRATA Rentals may assist the Guest in finding alternative accommodation, and penalties may apply to the Host according to our Host cancellation policy (details available in the Host terms).

Extenuating Circumstances

We may override the Host's cancellation policy if the cancellation is due to certain extenuating circumstances (e.g., natural disasters, government travel restrictions, serious illness). Documentation may be required. Please refer to our Extenuating Circumstances Policy for details.

How to Cancel

To cancel a booking, please log in to your PRATA Rentals account, go to your bookings section, and follow the cancellation instructions. Refunds will be processed according to the applicable policy and your original payment method.

This is a general overview. Always refer to the specific policy displayed for the property you are booking.
