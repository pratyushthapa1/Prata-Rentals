Help Center & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a property?

Use the search bar on the homepage or the 'All Properties' page. You can filter by location, property type, price range, and number of bedrooms/bathrooms (where available). Enter your criteria and click 'Search'.

How do I contact a Host?

On the property details page, you will find a 'Contact Owner' or 'Send Inquiry' button/form. Use this to send your questions directly to the Host or property manager. You may need to be logged in for certain contact options.

Is my booking confirmed immediately?

Booking confirmation processes may vary. Some properties might offer instant booking, while others require the Host to accept your request. The specific process will be outlined during the booking steps. You will receive a confirmation email once your booking is finalized.

Currently, the booking page simulates direct confirmation after filling out the form.

How does the cancellation policy work?

Cancellation policies (e.g., Flexible, Moderate, Strict) are set by the Host and displayed on the property listing. Please review the specific policy before booking. You can find more general information on our Cancellation Policy page.

How do I list my property?

If you are a property owner or manager interested in listing with us, please visit our Become a Host page for more information and to get started.

Need More Help?
